GUF Guf or Guf, real name Alexey Sergeevich Dolmatov (born September 23, 1979, Moscow, RSFSR, USSR) is a Russian rap artist, co-founder (together with Princip) and member of the group CENTR. Co-founder of the TsAO Records label, founder of the ZM Nation label. Winner of RMA awards, Rock Alternative Music Prize and others. Hip-Hop
SLIMUS Vadim Vitalievich Motylev (born January 21, 1981, Moscow, USSR), better known by his stage name Slim (Russian Slim), now Slimus (Russian Slimus) is a Russian rap artist, beatmaker and sound producer, former member of the groups " Smokescreen" and Centr. Co-founder of the TsAO Records label, founder of the AzimutZvuk label. RAP
Oxxymiron Oxxxymiron (Oksimiron; real name - Miron Yanovich Fedorov; born January 31, 1985, Leningrad) is a Russian rap artist, songwriter and public figure. He is one of the most commercially successful rap artists in Russia. His albums “The Eternal Jew” (2011) and “Gorgorod” (2015) made a significant contribution to the history of Russian rap. A number of researchers and critics classify the performers work as “intellectual rap”. RAP
